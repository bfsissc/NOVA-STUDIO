Getting Started
Sign up, log in, and explore your workspace
1
Create your account
On the login screen, click Create account. Enter your first name, last name, email, and a password (min. 6 characters). You can also sign up instantly using Sign up with Google — no password needed.
2
Sign in to your workspace
Enter your email and password, or use Continue with Google. First-time users can also click Continue with Demo Account to explore NOVA Studio without signing up.
3
Understand the layout
After login you'll see the Dashboard (your home base), a left Sidebar for navigation, and a top Navbar with search and your profile. All tools are accessible from the sidebar under Main and Tools.
4
Quick Actions on Dashboard
The Dashboard shows quick-access buttons for all major tools — Certificate Maker, College Portal, My Projects, My Profile, and Settings. Use the tool cards at the top for one-click access to your most-used features.
💡Tip: Your activity log on the Dashboard tracks everything you do — great for picking up where you left off.
🎓
Certificate Maker
Design and bulk-generate certificates using your own template
1
Open the Certificate Maker
Click 🎓 Certificates in the sidebar, or press + New Certificate from the Dashboard. The tool opens in a full canvas editor.
2
Upload your certificate template
In the left panel, click Upload Template and select a PNG or JPG image of your blank certificate design. The canvas will display it as your background.
3
Add text fields
Use the Add Text tool to place name, date, course, or any other fields on your certificate. Click on a text element on the canvas to select it, then drag it to position. Use the right panel to change font, size, colour, and alignment.
4
Upload your recipient CSV for bulk generation
Prepare a CSV file with columns matching your text fields (e.g. Name, Course, Date). Click Upload CSV in the Data panel, then map each CSV column to the corresponding field on your certificate.
5
Preview & Generate
Use the row navigation arrows to preview how each certificate will look. When satisfied, click Generate All — NOVA will create a certificate for every row in your CSV. You can then download them individually or as a ZIP.
💡Tip: Use the Fit to Screen button if your canvas looks too large or small. The canvas is A4 landscape (2480 × 1754 px) to match standard certificate print sizes.
⚠️Important: CSV column headers are case-sensitive. Make sure they exactly match the field names you defined in the editor.
📧
Certificate Mailer
Send generated certificates directly to recipients via Gmail
1
Open Cert Mailer
Click 📧 Cert Mailer in the sidebar. The mailer works after you have generated certificates using the Certificate Maker.
2
Upload your recipient CSV
The CSV must include a column named Email. The mailer will read each row and send a personalised certificate to that address. Supported columns: Name, Email, Course, Date, Organisation.
3
Customise your email subject & message
Edit the email subject line and body in the compose panel. You can use merge tags like {{Name}} or {{Course}} to personalise each email automatically.
4
Start sending
Click Send All. The Live Feed panel tracks the status of each email in real time — Pending → Generating → Sending → Sent (or Failed). You can monitor success rates and retry failed entries.
⚠️Gmail Daily Limit: Google limits email sends per day. NOVA tracks your daily send count in the Dashboard's Gmail Tracker. Avoid exceeding ~400–500 emails/day on a standard Gmail account to prevent suspension.
🏫
College Portal
Create and manage student portals with sharable verification links
1
Open College Portal
Click 🏫 College Portal in the sidebar. This is where you create and manage portals for different colleges or batches.
2
Create a new portal
Click + New Portal. Enter the portal name (e.g. "BFSI Batch 2025") and a short URL slug. The wizard will guide you through 4 steps: Name → Upload Logo → Upload Student Data → Done.
3
Upload student data via CSV or Excel
In step 3 of the wizard, upload an Excel (.xlsx) or CSV file with your student records. Required columns: Name, Roll No, Course, Grade/Marks. You can also manually add or edit rows in the table after upload.
4
Share the portal link
Once created, a unique URL is generated for your portal. Copy and share it with students — they can visit the link to view and verify their own records without needing a NOVA account.
5
Manage existing portals
From the portal list, you can Edit student data, Toggle the portal live/offline, Copy the shareable link, or Delete the portal entirely.
💡Tip: Set a portal to Offline if you need to update data before making it public again. Students will see a maintenance message until you re-enable it.
🔄
Data Sync
Real-time collaborative workbook rooms for collecting college data
1
Create a Workbook Room
Click + Create Room. Give it a descriptive name like "BFSI Batch 2025 · College Data". A unique room key is generated automatically.
2
Share the room key with colleges
Copy the room key and send it to your college coordinators. They can join the room by entering the key in their Data Sync page — no account required if you share the direct join link.
3
Collect & review data live
Each college fills their assigned sheet in real time. You can see updates as they happen. A Live badge appears in the sidebar when a room is active.
4
Export the compiled workbook
Once all colleges have submitted, click Export XLSX to download the full workbook with each college's data in a separate sheet — ready to use in your Certificate Maker or College Portal.

My Projects
Organise and revisit your saved certificate and design work
💾
Saving a project
In the Certificate Maker, click Save Project at any time. Give it a name — it will appear in My Projects for you to continue later.
🔁
Resuming a project
Go to 📁 My Projects in the sidebar. Click on any saved project to re-open it in the editor with all your settings intact.
🗑️
Deleting a project
Open a project from the list and click Delete. Projects moved to the bin can be restored before they are permanently removed.
📤
Exporting
From the Certificate Maker, use the Export button to download certificates as PNG images or a ZIP archive of all generated files.

Settings & Profile
Personalise your workspace and manage your account
🎨
Theme & Accent colour
Go to ⚙️ Settings → Appearance. Switch between Light and Dark mode, and choose an accent colour (Lime, Teal, Violet, Blue, Coral).
👤
Edit your profile
Go to 👤 My Profile to update your display name, profile photo, and role. Changes are saved to your account automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions
Quick answers to common questions
Is NOVA Studio free to use? ▶
Yes! NOVA Studio is free forever for core features including the Certificate Maker, College Portal, and Data Sync. Sign up with your email or Google — no credit card required.
What CSV format do I need for bulk certificates? ▶
Your CSV should have column headers in the first row (e.g. Name, Email, Course, Date). The header names must match the field names you set in the Certificate Maker. Save your file as UTF-8 CSV for best results.
Can I use my own certificate design? ▶
Absolutely. Upload any PNG or JPG image as your template — your company letterhead, branded design, or a blank frame. NOVA overlays the text fields on top of your image.
Why are some emails failing in the Cert Mailer? ▶
Common reasons: (1) the recipient's email address is invalid or misspelled in your CSV, (2) you have hit Gmail's daily sending limit, or (3) your Gmail account requires you to allow less-secure app access. Check the failed row in the Live Feed for a specific error message.
Can students verify their certificates online? ▶
Yes. When you generate certificates through the College Portal, each one gets a unique Certificate ID. Students can visit the Verify page and enter their ID to confirm authenticity — no login needed.
How do I add multiple colleges to a Data Sync room? ▶
Create a room and share the room key with all your coordinators. Each coordinator joins with the same key and fills their assigned sheet. You can see all sheets updating in real time from your host view.
I lost my work — can I recover it? ▶
If you saved your project before the issue, go to My Projects and look for it there. Projects deleted recently appear in the Bin and can be restored. NOVA auto-saves your progress periodically while you are working in the editor.
Still stuck? Reach out to the NOVA Studio team — we're happy to help.  ✦

function doPost(e) {
  try {
    var filename = e.parameter.filename || 'upload.zip';
    var b64      = e.parameter.filedata;
    var folderid = e.parameter.folderid || '';
    var bytes    = Utilities.base64Decode(b64);
    var blob     = Utilities.newBlob(bytes, 'application/zip', filename);
    var file     = folderid
      ? DriveApp.getFolderById(folderid).createFile(blob)
      : DriveApp.createFile(blob);
    return ContentService
      .createTextOutput(JSON.stringify({ok:true,url:file.getUrl()}))
      .setMimeType(ContentService.MimeType.JSON);
  } catch(err) {
    return ContentService
      .createTextOutput(JSON.stringify({ok:false,error:err.toString()}))
      .setMimeType(ContentService.MimeType.JSON);
  }
}
